About Me

Hi! I’m Carolina, 22 years old, born and raised in Portugal. I’m currently finishing my degree in Computer Science at FEUP, and somewhere along the way I realised that what I enjoy the most is building things that make sense, things that solve real problems or simply make someone’s life easier.

I’ve always been a curious person. I like understanding how things work, asking questions, and figuring things out on my own (though I also really enjoy working with others). Coding became a natural fit, it’s logical, creative, and always changing, which means I never stop learning.

Outside of tech, I’m a big fan of coffee-fueled mornings with music in the background, long walks, helping animals whenever I can, and getting lost in a good series (I have a soft spot for Big Bang Theory and Game of Thrones). I care about doing things with purpose, staying kind, and enjoying the process, not just the result.

This portfolio is a small window into what I’ve been working on, but also into who I am. Thanks for stopping by — I hope you like what you see! 🩷
